Welcome to PNC Cambodia

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia was the first project to be launched in 2005, in Phnom Penh. PNC offers two IT training programs in System and Network Administration (SNA) and Web Programming (WEP), based on a holistic educative approach including technical and soft skills, and a personal development program.

Our Values

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia is committed to making a difference through education and technology. Our values reflect our commitment to excellence, respect, responsibility, and commitment. These values guide our actions and decisions, and shape our approach to education and technology. More information about our values can be found in our Values section.

Commitment

Dedicated to making a difference through education and technology.

Respect

Valuing diversity and treating everyone with dignity and respect.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in education and personal development.

Responsibility

Taking ownership and being accountable for our actions and impact.
